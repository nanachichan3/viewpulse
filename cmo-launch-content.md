## YouTuber Outreach Strategy

### Tier 1: Micro-Influencers (1K-10K subscribers) — Best ROI
**Target criteria:**
- Active content creator (2+ videos/week)
- Engaged community (comment ratio > 1%)
- Niche: gaming, educational, tech reviews

**Outreach approach:**
1. Find creators discussing YouTube analytics pain points
2. DM with genuine insight from Viewpulse data
3. Offer free early access + feedback request
4. Ask for honest review if they find it valuable

**Example outreach message:**
> "Hey [Name], I noticed you mentioned struggling with YouTube analytics in your recent video. I built Viewpulse — a privacy-first analytics tool for creators. Would love for you to try it and give me honest feedback. No pressure to review, just curious if it's actually useful."

### Tier 2: Mid-Size Creators (10K-100K) — Reviews & Features
**Target criteria:**
- Content about YouTube growth, creator tools
- Reviews or "tools I use" content
- Collaborative (has done sponsorships or collabs)

**Outreach approach:**
1. Offer free access + small compensation for honest review
2. Provide exclusive data insights they can share
3. Co-create content (tutorial video featuring them)

**Priority channels to approach:**
- Channels that review creator tools (TubeBuddy, vidIQ alternatives)
- "How I grew my channel" YouTubers
- YouTube educators with engaged subscriber base

### Tier 3: Macro Influencers (100K+) — Awareness
**Only if good fit:**
- Creator economy focused
- Reviews/education content
- Privacy-conscious audience

**Approach:**
- Gift access + offer affiliate partnership
- Focus on authenticity, not scripted reviews

### Outreach Channels
1. **Twitter/X DM** — Primary, most creators responsive
2. **YouTube comment** — Engage with their content first, then DM
3. **Email** — If publicly listed, for more formal outreach
4. **Discord** — Many creator communities have tool channels
