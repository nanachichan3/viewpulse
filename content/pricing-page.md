## FAQ Section

**Q: Is my channel data safe?**
A: Yes. Viewpulse uses YouTube's official OAuth API — we only access the data YouTube authorizes. We never scrape, store, or sell your channel data. Your analytics stay yours.

**Q: What does "OAuth secure connect" mean?**
A: You connect your channel through YouTube's official login system. We receive only the read permissions you approve. No password sharing. No data harvesting. You can revoke access any time.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. Monthly plans cancel anytime. Annual plans — email us and we'll prorate your remaining balance.

**Q: What's included in the free tier?**
A: One channel, basic subscriber delta tracking, 7 days of history. It's the full Viewpulse experience at zero cost — no time limits, no feature gates on what we show you.

**Q: How do the comparative benchmarks work?**
A: We anonymize and aggregate data across creators in similar niches and subscriber ranges. You see how your CTR, watch time, and retention compare to peers — without ever exposing individual channel data.
