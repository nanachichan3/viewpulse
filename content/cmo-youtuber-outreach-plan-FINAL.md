## 🏘️ 3 Communities to Seed

### 1. r/NewTubers (200K members)
- **Post type:** "Viewpulse — I built a privacy-first YouTube analytics tool for small creators"
- **Timing:** Week 2–3 post-launch
- **Tone:** Builder, creator-first, transparent about product maturity
- **Note:** r/NewTubers is skeptical — be genuine, no hard sell. Lead with the problem, not the product.

### 2. r/YouTube (2.5M members)
- **Post type:** "What's your biggest frustration with YouTube Studio analytics?" (engagement bait)
- **Timing:** Week 1–2
- **Tone:** Community member, not promoter. Organically mention Viewpulse as a solution in replies (not top-level post).

### 3. YouTube Creator Discord Servers (3–5 servers)
- **Strategy:** Join as community member first (1–2 weeks of participation), then share Viewpulse in #tools or #analytics channels
- **Key servers:** Creator Collaboration servers, YouTube Creator Academy groups, CreatorMindset Discord
- **Note:** Follow each server's self-promotion rules strictly — shadow-banning risk is real
