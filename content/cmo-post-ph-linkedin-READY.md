Today we launched Viewpulse on Product Hunt.

It's a privacy-first YouTube analytics dashboard — no Google account required, no OAuth, 100% client-side. Your data stays in your browser, not on our servers.

---

**Why I built it**

Six months ago I was deep in YouTube analytics for channels in my niche. I wanted to understand what separated the growers from the plateau-ed.

The problem: YouTube Studio shows you *what* happened — views, watch time, a graph. It doesn't show you *why*.

No breakdown by video type. No traffic source analysis by topic. No real picture of when your specific audience is actually active.

Generic advice doesn't help when you have a real audience with real habits.

So I built Viewpulse to surface what was always there, just buried.

---

**The privacy piece**

Here's what bothered me about existing YouTube analytics tools: they all need Google OAuth. Another third-party with access to your channel data.

I didn't want that for myself. And I realized — most creators shouldn't have to accept that trade-off either.

Viewpulse runs entirely in your browser. Nothing stored on servers. No OAuth. No data sharing.

Your channel data stays yours.

---

**What it does**

- Audience retention curves broken down by video type (tutorial vs essay vs vlog)
- Traffic source breakdown by topic cluster
- Optimal posting windows tuned to your actual audience
- Cross-video performance comparison

First 500 creators get free early access → viewpulse.rachkovan.com

Would really appreciate any feedback. And if you know a creator who hates YouTube Studio's blind spots — please share. 🙏
