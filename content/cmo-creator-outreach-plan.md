## 🎯 Strategy Summary

Viewpulse is a privacy-first YouTube analytics dashboard for creators. Beta launch GTM = **20 small/mid YouTube creators (5k–100k subs) in the productivity/creator economy niche**, plus 3 online communities to seed.

**Core GTM thesis:** The creator economy self-reinforces. One happy early creator = 1 YouTube video = 50–500 new users.

## ✉️ Personalized Outreach Template

**Subject line:** [Channel Name] — Early access to Viewpulse (YouTube analytics that respect your data)

**Body:**

```
Hey [Name],

I've been following [Channel Name] for a while — especially your video on [specific topic they covered]. [Authentic 1-sentence observation about their content].

I built Viewpulse — a privacy-first YouTube analytics dashboard. It shows you CTR, watch time trends, subscriber delta, and video performance in one clean dashboard. No data collection, no third-party tracking — just your channel, your data.

I'm looking for 20 creators to test it before launch. You'd get:
- Free lifetime Pro access (no credit card)
- Direct line to the founder for feature requests
- Your name on our launch announcement if you want it

[Link to demo or landing page]

No pressure — just wanted to put it in front of creators who actually care about their analytics.

[Your name]
```

**Personalization tips:**
- Reference a specific video title or topic from their channel
- Keep it short — 3–4 sentences max
- Lead with value, not ask

## 🏘️ 3 Communities to Seed

### 1. r/NewTubers (200K members)
**Post type:** "Viewpulse — I built a privacy-first YouTube analytics tool for small creators"
**Timing:** Launch week
**Tone:** Builder, creator-first, transparent about product maturity
**Note:** r/NewTubers is skeptical of sponsorships — be genuine, no hard sell

### 2. r/YouTube (2.5M members)
**Post type:** "What's your biggest frustration with YouTube Studio analytics?" (engagement bait) → organically mention Viewpulse as a solution
**Timing:** Week 1–2
**Tone:** Community member, not promoter

### 3. YouTube Creator Discord servers (3–5 servers)
**Strategy:** Join as community member first, then share Viewpulse in #tools or #analytics channels
**Key servers:** Creator Collaboration servers, YouTube Creator Academy groups, MrBeast's creator Discord
**Note:** Follow each server's rules about self-promotion
