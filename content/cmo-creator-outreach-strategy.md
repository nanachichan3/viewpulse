## 🎯 Outreach Philosophy

The goal is NOT mass outreach. It's reaching creators who would genuinely find Viewpulse useful — and letting them discover it organically through a well-crafted HN post and targeted, personalized outreach.

**Core principles:**
- Personalize every message — reference something specific they've done
- Lead with curiosity, not promotion — "have you tried this?" beats "please check out my product"
- Offer value first: feedback requests are genuine, not transactional
- Be okay with silence — follow up once, max
- Timing: outreach AFTER the HN post is live (so you have something concrete to point to)

## 4. Response Templates (Post-Outreach)

### When a creator says they tried it:

> "Thanks for giving it a go, [CREATOR NAME]! I'd love to know what surprised you or what felt missing — I built this for myself first and genuinely iterate based on creator feedback.
>
> Any features you'd want added? I'm working on [upcoming feature] next."

### When a creator asks for a feature:

> "Great question — that's actually on my list. [What they asked about]. Not sure if it'll make the next sprint, but it's exactly the feedback I need.
>
> Want me to ping you when it's live? And if you ever want to chat more about your analytics approach, I'm always up for it."

### When a creator offers to cover/review it:

> "That would be amazing — I'd genuinely love that. [LINK] is the best way to get a feel for it. Happy to jump on a call or share any assets you need for a review.
>
> No rush, and I understand if it doesn't fit your content calendar. Just let me know either way — and thanks for even reaching out."

### When a creator gives critical feedback:

> "This is exactly what I needed to hear, thank you. [Specific feedback point] — you're right, that's a real gap. I [will fix it / am thinking about how to approach it].
>
> Would you be open to me following up once I've addressed it? Genuinely want to build something creators find useful."

### When asked "how does this compare to TubeBuddy/VidIQ?":

> "Good question. The big difference is philosophy — Viewpulse doesn't collect your data. No accounts, no tracking, your analytics stay yours. That means it won't do channel comparisons or AI recommendations — it's focused purely on giving you a clear look at your own numbers.
>
> I'd say: TubeBuddy/VidIQ if you want discovery features and don't mind data sharing. Viewpulse if you want a clean, privacy-first look at your own numbers without the noise."

## 6. What NOT To Do

| ❌ Don't | ✅ Instead |
|----------|-----------|
| Mass DM with the same copy | Personalize at least the opener per creator |
| Send DMs before HN post is live | Wait until there's something concrete to click |
| Follow up more than once | Accept silence gracefully |
| Ask for shares/reposts upfront | Ask for honest feedback first |
| Hard-sell in the first message | Lead with curiosity, not promotion |
| Send outreach to creators with 0 relevant content | Qualify before reaching out |
