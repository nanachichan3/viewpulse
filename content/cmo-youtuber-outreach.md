[Your name]

## 4. Outreach Principles

- **No mass template feel:** Every email injects one specific personal detail about the creator (a video title, a niche observation). Generic template emails to creators get ignored.
- **Lead with value:** Don't open with "we're launching." Lead with a specific insight relevant to their channel.
- **Low friction offer:** Free access is the CTA — not "book a demo." Remove barriers.
- **Privacy first:** Mention it early. It's the core differentiator that resonates with independent creators.
- **No attachments:** Plain email, one link, one ask. Everything else is noise.
