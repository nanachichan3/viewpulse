# Viewpulse — YouTuber Outreach Plan

**Project:** Viewpulse | **Type:** CMO Deliverable
**Stage:** Launch | **Priority:** High
**Date:** 2026-04-25

---

## Purpose

Execute a structured outreach campaign to YouTube creators (1K–500K subscribers) to:
1. Get product reviews / demonstrations on YouTube
2. Drive early waitlist signups via creator audiences
3. Seed organic social proof through trusted voices

**Coordination required:** CTO to provide demo access credentials for creators

---

## Target Creator Criteria

### Must-Have:
- YouTube channel focused on: YouTube growth, creator tips, content creation, analytics, or gaming/lifestyle where analytics matter
- 1K–500K subscribers (prioritize 10K–100K)
- Active (posted at least 3 videos in last 30 days)
- Audience engagement rate > 3% (comments/views ratio)

### Nice-to-Have:
- Posts on Twitter/X (creator Twitter presence = multiplier)
- Has done tool/product reviews before
- Audience that overlaps with Viewpulse ICP (creators who care about analytics)

### Excluded:
- Channels focused on drama, commentary, or non-creator topics
- Creators with known anti-tool or anti-SEO content
- Channels with buy-audience (giveaway-heavy)

## Outreach Sequence

### Tier A: Warm Introduction (Priority 1)
**Approach:** DM via Twitter/YouTube community tab with warm intro
**Template:**
> Hey [Name]!
>
> [Common connection — if available: mutual follower, same platform community, recent video they made that I engaged with]
>
> I'm [Yev] — I just launched Viewpulse, a YouTube analytics dashboard built for creators who want more than what YouTube Studio shows them.
>
> I genuinely use your content to think about [specific topic from their video]. Your video on [topic] was especially [specific compliment].
>
> I'd love to give you early access and get your take. No obligations — just happy to share if you think your audience would find it useful.
>
> [Demo link or access code]
>
> Either way, keep doing what you're doing — [specific thing about their content]. 🎬

### Tier B: Cold Outreach (Priority 2)
**Approach:** Email or Twitter DM with personalized cold outreach
**Email Template:**
> **Subject:** A tool built for creators like you — Viewpulse early access
>
> Hey [Name],
>
> You don't know me, but I've been following your content for a bit — especially your video on [topic]. The point about [specific insight from their video] really stuck with me.
>
> I built Viewpulse — a YouTube analytics dashboard that shows you what YouTube Studio hides: audience retention curves by video type, traffic source breakdowns by topic, optimal posting windows for YOUR specific audience.
>
> Most creators are using generic "post at 4 PM on Fridays" advice. Viewpulse shows you YOUR actual patterns.
>
> I'd love to give you free lifetime access in exchange for an honest take — a 5-minute demo video, a community post, or just your Twitter thoughts. Zero pressure to say anything specific.
>
> Here's access: [link]
>
> Let me know what you think. And if it's not a fit, totally get it — but I think your audience would especially benefit from the retention curve data.
>
> — Yev
>
> P.S. Happy to jump on a 15-min call if you'd rather talk it through first.

## Demo Access Plan (Coordination with CTO)

CTO to prepare:
1. **Dedicated creator access tier** — full dashboard access, pre-populated with sample channel data so creators can see the value immediately without connecting their own channel
2. **Personalized demo accounts** — for top-tier creators (Tier A), create accounts with their own channel data pre-loaded so they see real results
3. **Access codes** — unique codes per creator to track which channels drove the most signups
4. **Demo video access** — short Loom/screen recording walkthrough for creators who want to see it before connecting their channel

> Amazing — excited to get your take! Here's how this works:

> 1. Connect your YouTube channel (30 seconds via Google OAuth)
> 2. Explore the dashboard — retention curves, traffic sources, best posting windows
> 3. Let me know what you think — happy to jump on a call or just get your Twitter threads/dm
>
> No obligations on content. Just honest feedback. If you DO want to make something, here's what resonates with your audience:
> - "What YouTube Studio hides vs. what Viewpulse shows"
> - "How to find YOUR optimal posting time (not the generic advice)"
> - "I tried this for [X] and [result]"
>
> Would also love to feature your feedback on our launch page if you're into it. 🎬

### If They Decline:
> Totally understand — thanks for being honest. If you ever change your mind or want to see it later, here's the link: [link]
>
> And seriously — keep making [their content niche]. It's needed.

## Key Message Points for Creators

**What Viewpulse shows that YouTube Studio doesn't:**
1. Audience retention curves by video type (not just overall)
2. Traffic source breakdown by topic (not just aggregate)
3. YOUR actual best posting windows (not generic advice)
4. Click-through rate by thumbnail style
5. Watch time patterns that predict viral potential
