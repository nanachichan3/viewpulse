# Viewpulse — Distribution Plan

**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Stage:** Post-MVP — Distribution  
**Owner:** CMO  
**Target MRR:** $1,000 (Phase 1)

---

## Vision

Viewpulse becomes the privacy-first analytics tool for independent YouTube creators who are frustrated with YouTube Studio's limitations and don't want to be tracked by third-party tools that sell their data.

**Positioning:** "See your real stats. We don't track you."

---

## Revenue Model

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Connect 1 channel, 7d/28d view data, 20 videos |
| **Pro** | $9/mo | All date ranges, unlimited videos, export-ready data, priority API |
| **Studio** | $29/mo | 5 channels, comparative analytics (post-MVP), dedicated support |

> Note: Pricing to be validated with creator surveys before any public launch. These are starting hypotheses.

---

## Target: $k MRR

**Phase 1 goal: $1,000 MRR** = ~111 Pro subscribers

This is deliberately modest:
- It's achievable without a large marketing budget
- It's a signal that real creators are willing to pay
- It funds continued development while we find PMF

**Phase 2:** $3,000 MRR (~330 Pro subscribers)  
**Phase 3:** $10,000 MRR (requires channel diversification)

## Distribution Channels

### Channel 1: YouTube Creator Communities

**Where:** Reddit (r/newtube, r/youtube, r/contentcreator), Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Discord servers for YouTube growth (VidIQ community, TubeBuddy community, Roberto Blake's community)

**Strategy:**
- Engage authentically — answer questions about YouTube analytics without pitching
- Share Viewpulse as a tool when relevant (e.g., "I built this to see my real CTR")
- No spam, no drive-by promotion

**Content format:**
- Short form: Twitter/X threads about interesting YouTube stats patterns
- Long form: Blog posts on "X things YouTube Studio doesn't tell you"

**Responsible:** CMO (primary), CEO (personal network)

---

## What We Need Before Launch

### Blockers

1. **No payment integration** — Can't convert without Stripe/LemonSqueezy
2. **No landing page** — First-time visitors have no context
3. **No email capture** — Can't build waitlist for launch blast
4. **No creator validation** — Haven't asked creators if they'd pay for this
