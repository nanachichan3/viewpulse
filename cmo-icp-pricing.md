# Viewpulse — ICP & Pricing Tier Strategy

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: YouTube Creators 10K–100K Subscribers

**Profile:**
- 25–40 years old
- Creates content as side hustle or early full-time career
- Uses YouTube Studio daily but frustrated with limited metrics
- Already uses TubeBuddy or VidIQ (and pays for it) but feels it's bloated/misses key data
- Cares about: watch time patterns, CTR, avg view duration, audience retention
- Frustrated that YouTube shows views but buries insight metrics

**Behavioral signals:**
- Searched "how to improve avg view duration YouTube"
- Subscribed to "creator economy" newsletters
- Active in YouTube creator Discord servers or subreddits
- Uses 2+ analytics or SEO tools for YouTube

**Pain points:**
- YouTube Studio doesn't show CTR or avg view duration trends
- TubeBuddy/VidIQ are expensive ($99+/month for decent features) and feel bloated
- No privacy-first option — most tools scrape or require broad permissions
- Can't see channel-wide patterns across videos (which thumbnails work better, which topics resonate)

**Willingness to pay:**
- Currently paying $15–$50/month for existing tools
- Willing to switch if: better data, cleaner UX, privacy-first, fair price
- MVP price sensitivity: will pay $9–$15/month for meaningful metric improvements

---

### Secondary ICP: New Creators (1K–10K Subscribers)

**Profile:**
- Early career creator, <2 years on YouTube
- Wants to understand what's working vs what's not
- Doesn't have budget for TubeBuddy/VidIQ yet
- Looking for free or very cheap tool to get started

**Opportunity:** Capture early, grow with them. Free tier → paid tier as channel grows.

---

### Tertiary ICP: Brand/Agency Managing Multiple Channels

**Profile:**
- Manages 3+ YouTube channels (own brand or client channels)
- Needs multi-channel overview
- Willing to pay higher price for team access + multi-channel dashboard

**Note:** This ICP is for future tiers, not MVP launch.

## 3-Tier Pricing Structure

### Tier 1 — Explorer (Free)
**Price:** $0/month
**Target:** New creators 1K–10K who want basic analytics

**Features:**
- Connect 1 YouTube channel via OAuth
- View all basic metrics (views, CTR, avg view duration, watch time)
- 30-day data history
- Sort/filter videos by any metric
- Exportable performance table (CSV)

**Limits:**
- No real-time data (24h refresh delay)
- No 90-day trend view
- No comparison tools

**Conversion path:** Upsell to Creator tier when they need historical trends

---

### Tier 2 — Creator ($12/month)
**Target:** Serious creators 10K–100K who need full analytics

**Features:**
- Everything in Explorer
- Real-time data refresh
- 12-month data history
- Avg view duration trend chart (7/28/90 day views)
- CTR trend chart over time
- Subscriber delta pattern view
- Watch time heatmap per video
- Customizable metric dashboard (pin your 3 key metrics)
- Email digest of weekly performance

**Limits:**
- 1 YouTube channel max

**Conversion path:** Upsell to Pro when managing client channels

---

### Tier 3 — Pro ($29/month)
**Target:** Agency/brand managing own channels or 2+ channels

**Features:**
- Everything in Creator
- Up to 5 YouTube channels
- Multi-channel comparison dashboard
- Priority support (48h response)
- Priority feature requests

**Limits:**
- Team access (future consideration)

---

### Enterprise (Custom — Future)
- Unlimited channels
- Team access + permissions
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated support

## Pricing Strategy Notes

**Launch day offer:** Creator tier free for first 100 signups (6-month lock-in at $12)
- Creates urgency for waitlist conversion
- Captures serious early adopters who'll provide feedback

**Price anchoring:** Don't compete with TubeBuddy ($99+/mo). Lead with "TubeBuddy quality, $12/month" narrative.

**Yearly discount:** 2 months free (~$100/year vs $144/year) — standard SaaS anchor.
